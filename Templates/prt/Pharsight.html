The Solution: Actionable Insights from Complex Data

As a lead developer, I played a pivotal role in engineering Pharsight, a web application tailored for medicine producers (owners, founders, CEOs). This platform empowers them to analyze critical market data – including raw materials, market size, competitor analysis, and detailed medicine specifications – facilitating informed business planning. Pharsight provides subscription-based access to these invaluable market insights, transforming complex data into strategic advantages.

Driving Business Logic & Scalability

My contribution was central to developing the core business logic, encompassing over 20,000 lines of code dedicated to robust market data analysis. I designed and implemented a high-performance RESTful API using Django Rest Framework, ensuring seamless and efficient communication between the backend and a responsive ReactJS frontend. Furthermore, I integrated MongoDB to manage and store large, complex datasets, critical for comprehensive market analysis. The entire project was meticulously deployed on AWS, guaranteeing superior scalability and a resilient cloud infrastructure for continuous service availability.

Core Technologies & Architecture


  • Python/Django (Backend Logic)
  • MongoDB (Scalable Data Storage)
  • React.js (Dynamic User Interface)
  • Django REST Framework (Powerful APIs)
  • AWS (Cloud Deployment & Scalability)
