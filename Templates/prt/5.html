What is about this project

In this project, I aimed to boost users' social presence on Instagram through automation. I utilized Django commands to perform various actions such as creating Instagram accounts, performing random actions, updating profiles, uploading photos, and engaging with other users. The primary objective was to create a system that mimicked real human behavior on Instagram. To achieve this, I implemented various functionalities such as obtaining OTPs for account creation and verification using the Getsms service, updating profile details to make it more authentic, and performing targeted engagement with other users.

Challanges We Have Faced

One of the major challenges in this project was to emulate real human behavior on Instagram. The platform has strict rules and regulations, and even a minor deviation from the standard behavior could result in account suspension. To tackle this challenge, I had to ensure that all the scripts were optimized to behave like a real user and avoid any suspicious activities. The other challenge was to provide a comprehensive report of all the activities performed by the system. To overcome this, I created a Django command that generated an overall report and set crontabs to keep track of all the actions.
